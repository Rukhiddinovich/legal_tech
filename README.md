# ⚖️ LegalTech Ecosystem

> An open-source LegalTech platform that connects clients with verified lawyers, automates legal document generation, and simplifies access to legal services using AI.

![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-green)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-Active%20Development-blue)
![Open Source](https://img.shields.io/badge/Open%20Source-Yes-success)

## 📖 About

LegalTech Ecosystem is an open-source platform designed to modernize legal services in Uzbekistan. The platform replaces fragmented communication through messaging apps with a secure, transparent, and AI-powered legal marketplace.

Our mission is to make legal assistance more accessible, affordable, and efficient for individuals, businesses, and legal professionals.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👨‍⚖️ Lawyer Marketplace
- Verified lawyer profiles
- Legal specialization categories
- Smart rating & review system
- Online / Offline availability

### 💬 Secure Consultation
- Fixed-price legal consultations
- Encrypted real-time chat
- Audio & Video consultations
- Consultation timer
- Session extension support

### 🤖 AI Legal Assistant
- AI-powered legal guidance
- Legal document explanation
- Intelligent question answering
- Future LLM integrations

### 📄 Legal Document Generator
Generate professional legal documents including:
- Contracts
- Applications
- Agreements
- Official forms

Documents are generated automatically from user inputs and exported as PDF.

### 🧮 Legal Fee Calculator
- Court fee calculator
- Notary fee calculator
- Government fee estimation

### 💼 Business Subscription (SaaS)
Businesses can subscribe to monthly plans including:
- Legal consultations
- Document generation
- Priority support
- Company legal dashboard

## 💳 Payment System

Supported payment providers:

- Click
- Payme
- Uzum Bank
- International Cards

Revenue Split:

- 90% → Lawyer
- 10% → Platform

## 🔒 Security

Security is one of our highest priorities.

Features include:

- End-to-end encrypted messaging
- Contact hiding
- Anti-contact sharing detection
- Fraud prevention
- Personal data protection
- Compliance with Uzbekistan legislation
